Cake Smash Sessions

I can't think of a  better way to celebrate your child's birthday than smashing a delicious cake. 
The actual cake smash shoots are child led, this means we go at your children's pace. If they are getting bored we move on.  If they want a break, we take a break.  

I start with taking photographs of your child while still clean, so we get some beautiful shots in his/her lovely outfit. 

After that I bring a cake and encourage child to dig in. Some kids go all in, and some try to be a bit more civilized ;) Some kids prefer to eat with a spoon, some eat straight with their mouth. All options are fun to watch and capture. They already show their little personalities. 

Next stage is bath time, yes in the studio! They can have a good splash and get clean. I offer milk baths with fresh flowers, or tasty baths with fruit: strawberries, oranges, grapefruits and lemons. 

I can order a cake matching your child's cake smash theme from a local bakery for additional £30 -£40, or you can bring your own birthday cake.

 Balloon arches are available to book for an extra £50 but this has to be booked in advance as it's beeing outsourced from a loocal party organizing company. 

I always advise parents to take some spare clothes for themselves as kids at this age are very mobile and interested in everything which surrounds them, so they tend to run away from the set. Parents usually help me with keeping them in place and supervising while they are in the water. 

Cake smash sessions can be booked for kids all ages, but the most popular ones are for 1st, 2nd and 3rd birthday.

What's included in the cake smash session

My standard Cake smash and splash set up includes:
- 6 balloons in colours of your choice
- pom-poms in similar colours
- bunting in similar colours
- backdrop of your choice: white, grey, black, Paris, blue polka dot
- fresh flowers or fruit for milk bath
- numbers, letters
- props like vintage cars, rocking horses, wooden beds, flower tent
- I have a selection of beautiful outfits for you to choose from starting with tutus, dresses, rompers, headbands and for boys cute pants, suspenders, bow ties and hats
- cake is not included in the price due to food hygiene/intolerance and allergy reasosns 

I love to be creative and I want your child to have the best experience ever so If you have a different vision and idea in your head feel free to let me know and I will be happy to create it for you. 

I work with local bisnesses which are more than happy to help with preparing bespoke set ups for you like balloon arches, balloon numbers, hot air ballons, flower walls , personalised outfits and many more. Sky is the limit!
