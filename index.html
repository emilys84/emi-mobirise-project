Hello!

My name is Emilia Reilly and I am so happy you took a moment to stop by my site.

I create bespoke and exquisite images which will be tailored to your needs. It could be your own vision, or you can just leave everything in my hands and I will create something special for you. 

I am your storyteller. I am here to capture the story of your newborn baby, celebrate his/hers first birthday during the messy cake smash and milk bath or capture emotional and stylish fine art portraits when they grow older. I am also very happy to see YOU at my photo shoots: beautiful and confident. I love to make sure all your images are special and timeless.

I believe in taking photographs that make you feel wonderful in your own skin and in your own life. I feel privileged to be able to make you feel great about yourself by capturing your inner beauty in images that you can treasure forever and will always act as a reminder to you of how beautiful you truly are!

Photo shoots take place in my home studio based in Giltbrook, Nottingham or in any other outdoor location. I know a number of lovely places in the neighbourhood. I cover Eastwood, Heanor, Kimberley, Nottingham, Ilkeston, Derby, Mansfield but can travel to any other locations.

Let start your own story and save your memories in stunning pictures!

ABOUT ME

My name is Emilia and I am a mother of two lovely kids age 4 and 7. I am a qualified teacher but decided to choose a different path after all. My passion to photography started with the birth of my first child. I really wanted to capture his childhood in beatiful and unique way. Thanks to that he has lots of gorgeous photographs to treasure for many years to come and for us to remember his beautiful childhood. With my second child my passion developed to something deeper and I started learning photography professionally. So here I am trying to make memories for all of you because every person has a different story to tell. Let me tell your story.
